Our 8-Step Program

Early Season

  • January - February
  • Pre-emergent spray for control of crabgrass and other grassy weeds
  • Spray for broadleaf weeds as needed

Spring

  • March - April
  • Additional pre-emergent for extended grassy weed control
  • Spray for broadleaf weeds as needed

Late Spring

  • April - May
  • Fertilization for green up and root development
  • Spray for early weeds such as poa annua and firewood

Early Summer

  • June - July
  • Fertilization to maintain green and healthy turf
  • Weed control for weeds such as crabgrass and nutgrass as needed

Summer

  • July - August
  • Fertilization to extend color through summer
  • Weed control for weeds such as crabgrass and nutgrass as needed

Fall

  • August - October
  • Pre-emergent for control of winter weeds such as poa annua

Winter

  • October - November
  • Pre-emergent for control of winter weeds such as onions, oxalis and spurge
  • Broadleaf weed control as needed

Lime

  • November - December
  • Lime applied to raise soil pH, so next year's fertilization is more effective
  • Broadleaf weed control is needed
